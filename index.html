About the Books

Game of Thrones is the first book in the popular in the series A song of Ice and Fire by George R. R. Martin. The book was first published in 1996.

There are five books currently written in the series. Others include A Clash of Kings, A Storm of Swords, A Feast for Crows, and A Dance with Dragons. There are two more books in the series in production.

The main story follows a struggle for power after the death of the King of Westeros, a fictional kingdom in the series.

The series has sold over 24 million copies in North America, and it has spawned an HBO series.
